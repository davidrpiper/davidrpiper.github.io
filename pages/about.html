Welcome to my corner of the Internet. This is where I balance my content consumption with creation.

The conventional wisdom these days is to create an online presence with a very narrow or specialised focus to appeal to people with specific interests. I want to deliberately do the opposite. This blog will be a wide-ranging collection of content, with the only common thread that everything comes from me.

Consequently, not every post here will be interesting to everyone. And that's fine.

There's no subscribe button, but if you enjoy what I do, there's an RSS (Atom) feed. If there's only a single post in the entire reel that's of any interest at all, that's great! I hope you found it valuable.

If you want to discuss a post, feel free to link it wherever you want to discuss it. My email address is below if you'd like my input.

What do you do?

One goal of this blog is to be completely separate from my professional life. That said, what I do will of course influence what I write, and in some cases that context will be useful. So, briefly:

  • I have a degree in Mathematical and Computer Sciences, majoring in Computer Science.
  • I have a degree in Chemical Engineering, with first class honours.
  • Under the business name 44Apps, I built educational iPad and iPhone apps (iOS7-era) for high school Chemistry and Physics students.
  • I've worked at two well-known Australian software companies.
  • I still work at one of them. I'm currently a full-stack web developer.

If you'd like a proper CV, my email address is at the bottom of the page.
